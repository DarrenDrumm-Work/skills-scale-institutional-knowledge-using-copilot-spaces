### Engagement Level Definitions
- **Manage Closely** (High Influence, High Interest): Engage regularly, involve in key decisions
- **Keep Satisfied** (High Influence, Low Interest): Keep informed of major decisions, address concerns promptly
- **Keep Informed** (Low Influence, High Interest): Regular updates, leverage as advocates
- **Monitor** (Low Influence, Low Interest): Minimal communication, monitor for changes
